⚙️ ONE MORE QUICK SETUP:

  Get your Notion database ID:
    1. Open your Notion workspace
    2. Go to student database
    3. Look at URL: https://notion.so/xxxxxxxxxxxxx?v=yyyy
    4. Copy the ID (the long string before ?)
    5. Edit .env file:
       NOTION_DATABASE_ID=xxxxxxxxxxxxx

  Then Hermes can sync your database!
